# Unit 2 Task 46 - Guess my number with play again

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-46-solution/)

---


Combine loops: an **outer** loop asks if the player wants another round (`y` / `n`). An **inner** loop plays **one** full game (secret 1–20, too high / too low until correct).

**Skills:** nested `while`, `break` only inner loop, string check for play again.

Keep the range **1–20** so rounds are quick.
