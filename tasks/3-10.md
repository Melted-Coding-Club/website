# Unit 3 Task 10 - Reading list slices

[← Unit 3 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/3/) - [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/3-10-solution/)

---



Given a list of at least **six** integers in your code (e.g. chapter IDs), print:

- The **first three** items (slice).
- The **last two** items (slice with negative indexes or `len`).
- The **middle** three items starting at index 2 (i.e. slice `[2:5]` if your list is long enough).

**Skills:** slicing `[start:end]`, `[ :3]`, `[-2:]`.

**Example list:** `[10, 20, 30, 40, 50, 60]`
