# Unit 2 Task 4 - Counting even raffle tickets

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-4-solution/)

---


At a raffle, each ticket has a whole number. You type ticket numbers as they are sold. When you are done, you enter **-1** (not a real ticket).

Write a program that:

- Reads integers until **-1** is entered.
- **Counts** how many of the numbers (other than -1) were **even** (use `% 2 == 0`).
- Prints how many even ticket numbers you saw.

**Skills:** `while`, `if`, `%`, counters.
