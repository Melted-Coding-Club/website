# Unit 2 Task 7 - Freezing day on the farm

Farmers worry about frost. Read **one** morning temperature in **Celsius**.

- If the temperature is **0 or below**, print that pipes or puddles **may freeze**.
- Otherwise print that it is **above freezing**.

**Skills:** one `if` / `else`, one `input`, `float` or `int`.
