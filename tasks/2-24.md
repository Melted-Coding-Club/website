# Unit 2 Task 24 - Guess with seven tries

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-24-solution/)

---


Pick a secret number from **1 to 50** with `random.randint(1, 50)`. The player has **at most 7** guesses.

- If they guess right, print a success message and stop.
- If they run out of guesses without finding it, reveal the secret and print **`You lose`**.

Still give **Too high** / **Too low** hints after each wrong guess.

**Skills:** `for` over range(7) with `break`, or `while` with guess count.
