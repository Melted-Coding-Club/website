# Unit 4 Task 3 - Price after discount

[← Unit 4 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/4-functions/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/4-functions/3-solution/)

---

Write `discounted_price(price, percent_off)`:
- `price` is the original price (a number, e.g. `float`).
- `percent_off` is how many **percent** to take off (e.g. `25` means 25% off).
- **Return** the new price after the discount.

After the function, print the result of at least two test calls (you can hard-code the numbers; `input` is optional).

**Skills:** `return`, arithmetic with percentages.
