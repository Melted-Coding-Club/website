# Unit 4 Task 12 - Three addition questions

[← Unit 4 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/4-functions/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/4-functions/12-solution/)

---

Write **`ask_addition()`**:
- Choose two **random** integers from **1** to **12** inclusive (`import random` at the top of your file).
- Ask the user `What is a + b?` (show the actual numbers) and read an integer.
- **Return** `True` if their answer is correct, otherwise `False`.

Write **`main()`** that:
- Runs **three** rounds (call `ask_addition` three times).
- Counts how many were correct.
- Prints the final score, e.g. `Score: 2 / 3`

At the very end of the file, call `main()`.

**Skills:** multiple functions, `return` and `random`, a small driver program.
