# Unit 2 Task 37 - Guess the product

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-37-solution/)

---


The computer picks two secret numbers **A** and **B** from **1 to 10**. The player tries to guess **A × B**.

Loop until the guess is correct. Each wrong guess should say **too low** or **too high** compared to the real product.

**Skills:** `random`, `while`, comparisons.
