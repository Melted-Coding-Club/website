# Unit 2 Task 22 - Savings goal

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-22-solution/)

---


You start with **$0**. Ask for a **goal** amount (float) and then repeatedly ask how much you **save this week** (float). Add each week’s savings to your balance.

Stop when your balance is **greater than or equal to** the goal. Then print how many **weeks** it took and the **final** balance.

**Skills:** `while` with a condition on a running total, week counter.
