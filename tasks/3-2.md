# Unit 3 Task 2 - Lap times summary

[← Unit 3 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/3/) - [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/3-2-solution/)

---



A go-kart driver has **five** lap times stored in a list of floats (you can put numbers in the code). Print:

- The **fastest** lap (smallest time) - you may use `min(lap_times)`.
- The **average** lap (sum divided by how many laps) - use `sum(lap_times)` and `len(lap_times)`.

**Skills:** lists of numbers, `min`, `sum`, `len`.
