# Unit 2 Task 17 - Five-question mental maths quiz

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/17-solution/)

---


A tutor quiz uses **five** rounds. For each round:

- Pick two random integers from **1 to 10** (use `random.randint`).
- Ask: `What is A + B? `
- If the user’s answer is correct, add 1 to a **score**.

At the end, print the score out of 5.

**Skills:** `for` loop, `random`, comparing integers from `input`.
