# Unit 2 Task 11 - Strawberry stand checkout

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/11-solution/)

---


You sell strawberries. Ask once for the **cost** (float). Then ask how much **money** the customer gives you.

- If the money is **less** than the cost, print **`No sale`** and ask again for a new amount (keep going until they pay enough).
- If money **equals** cost, print **`Thank you for your payment`**.
- If money is **greater** than cost, print change as: `Your change is: $X` where **X** is rounded to **1 decimal place** (use `round(..., 1)`).

Use a loop, and `if` / `elif` / `else` as needed.

**Skills:** `while`, floats, `round`.
