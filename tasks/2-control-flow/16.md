# Unit 2 Task 16 - ATM PIN (three tries)

[← Unit 2 tasks]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/) · [Solution]({{ site.baseurl }}/tasks/2-control-flow/16-solution/)

---


The correct PIN is **`2468`**. The user has **at most 3** attempts.

- On a correct PIN, print **`Welcome`** and stop.
- On a wrong PIN, say how many tries are left (or that the card is locked).
- After **3** wrong tries, print **`Card locked`** and stop (do not keep asking).

You can use a `for` loop over three attempts with `break`, or a `while` with a counter.

**Skills:** loop + `if` / `else`, early exit.
